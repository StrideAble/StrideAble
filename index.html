The Challenge

Mobility Aids Are Passive.
People Deserve More.

Every year, millions of older adults and mobility device users face preventable dangers — not because of their conditions, but because their tools haven't kept up with the times.

⚠️

Fall Risk

Falls are the leading cause of injury-related hospital admissions among older adults. Standard mobility aids offer no active fall prevention or detection.

🚧

Obstacle Danger

Curbs, uneven floors, and unexpected hazards are invisible threats. Traditional devices provide no warning until it's too late.

📡

Caregiver Blind Spots

Caregivers and family members have no real-time visibility into a loved one's safety, location, or movement patterns throughout the day.

📊

No Health Tracking

Vital mobility and health data goes uncaptured. Clinicians and caregivers lack the insights needed to prevent incidents before they happen.

The Solution

One Attachment.
Total Transformation.

StrideAble is a compact, AI-powered attachment that fastens directly onto existing mobility devices using velcro — no replacement required. In seconds, a standard walker or cane becomes an intelligent, connected safety system.

  • Works with your existing device — no replacement needed
  • Front-facing AI camera scans the path ahead
  • Haptic handle alerts before obstacles are reached
  • Automatic caregiver notifications on fall detection
  • First responders alerted if user doesn't get up in 3 minutes
  • Emergency SOS button sends location instantly
  • Health and activity monitoring built in
Intelligent By Design

8 Powerful Features. One Smart Attachment.

Mount the device facing forward — the front-facing AI camera and sensors detect obstacles directly in the user's path.

📷

Geometric AI Camera

Front-facing camera uses depth analysis to identify obstacles, steps, and hazards before the user reaches them.

🏃

Motion Sensors

High-precision accelerometers and gyroscopes track gait patterns, step frequency, and overall mobility trends.

📳

Haptic Feedback

Vibrations through the handle intensify as obstacles get closer — light at 3 ft, increasing to a strong pulse at 1 ft.

❤️

Health Monitoring

Continuous tracking of activity levels and mobility metrics, shareable with caregivers and healthcare providers.

🛡️

Fall Detection AI

AI detects falls in real time. Caregivers are alerted immediately. If the user doesn't get up within 3 minutes, first responders are automatically contacted.

🆘

Emergency SOS

One press of the SOS button instantly sends the user's live location and an ambulance request to emergency services.

📱

Caregiver App

Real-time visibility into location, activity, health insights, and safety alerts — without compromising user independence.

🤖

AI Companion

Medication reminders, gentle encouragement, and compassionate check-ins. Learns user preferences over time.

Simple Setup

Ready in 4 Steps.

01

Attach with Velcro

Secure StrideAble to any walker, cane, or scooter using the velcro attachment. No tools required — under 60 seconds.

02

Face It Forward

Orient the device so the front-facing camera and sensors point outward in the direction of travel.

03

Feel the Feedback

The handle vibrates with proximity-based intensity alerts when obstacles are detected. Automatic and non-intrusive.

04

Stay Connected

Caregivers receive real-time safety alerts, location sharing, activity insights, and health updates through the companion app.
